About This Project

This project implements two regression models for car price prediction:

  • Logistic Regression: Classifies whether a car's price is high or low based on multiple factors.
  • Polynomial Regression: Predicts the exact car price using Car Age as the independent variable.

Both models are trained on a dataset of car information including age, mileage, brand, fuel type, and transmission.
